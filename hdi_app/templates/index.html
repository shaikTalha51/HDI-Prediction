One number, three lives' worth of data.

The Human Development Index distills how long people live, how much they learn, and how well they can afford to live, into a single comparable score from 0 to 1. This tool predicts that score for any country profile using a regression model trained on life expectancy, schooling, and income data.

How the prediction works

1. Inputs

Life expectancy, mean & expected years of schooling, GNI per capita (PPP $).

2. Model

A Linear Regression model trained on a standardized feature set.

3. Output

A predicted HDI score (0–1) plus its Low / Medium / High / Very High tier.
